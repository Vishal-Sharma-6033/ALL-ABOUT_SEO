# 🧠 SEO Fundamentals

---

## What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving website visibility in search engines.

Goals:
- increase organic traffic
- improve rankings
- generate leads
- increase conversions
- build brand authority

---

## How Google Understands Websites

Google uses:
- crawlers
- indexing systems
- semantic analysis
- user signals
- ranking systems

Google evaluates:
- relevance
- authority
- content quality
- backlinks
- internal linking
- page speed
- user engagement

# 🔥 Topic Clusters

Topic clusters are groups of related pages connected around one major topic.

---

## Example

```txt

 ├── Compress PDF
 ├── Merge PDF
 ├── OCR PDF
 ├── AI PDF Editor
 └── Best AI PDF Editors
```

---

## Benefits

- improves semantic relevance
- builds topical authority
- improves crawlability
- increases rankings
- improves user navigation

# 🔗 Internal Linking

Internal linking connects pages inside your website.

---

## Example

```html
<a href="/compress-pdf">Compress PDF</a>
```

---

## Internal Linking Flow

```txt

 ↓
Tool Page
 ↓
Related Tool
 ↓
Category Page
```

---

## Benefits

- passes authority
- improves indexing
- improves crawl depth
- improves rankings
- reduces bounce rate

# 🌐 Backlinks

Backlinks are links from external websites pointing to your website.

Google treats backlinks as trust signals.

---

# 💎 Types of Backlinks

---

## 1. Editorial Backlinks

Natural links from blogs/news websites.

---

## 2. Guest Post Backlinks

Links from articles written on other websites.

---

## 3. Directory Backlinks

Links from startup/SaaS directories.

---

## 4. HARO Backlinks

Reporter request links.

---

## 5. Forum Backlinks

Community and discussion backlinks.

---

## 6. Profile Backlinks

Links from social/profile pages.

---

# 🚀 High Quality Backlinks

Strong backlinks come from:
- news websites
- universities
- government sites
- relevant blogs
- authority SaaS companies

---

# ❌ Bad Backlinks

Avoid:
- spam comments
- PBN spam
- auto-generated links
- irrelevant sites
- link farms

# ⚡ Technical SEO

# 📋 Technical SEO Checklist

---

## Core Web Vitals

## Schema Markup

Add:
